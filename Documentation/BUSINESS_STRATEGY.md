## 💵 Pricing Strategy

**Includes:**
- 1,000 requests/month
- 1 firewall

- 30-day audit log retention
- Community support (Discord/Forum)
- API access

**Limitations:**
- No custom rules
- No team features
- No export functionality
- QueryShield branding on reports

**Goal:** Acquire users, build community, convert 10% to paid

**Expected Users:** 5,000+ in Year 1

### Pricing Psychology

**Why $49 for Pro?**
- Below $50 barrier (feels affordable)
- No C-level approval needed at most companies
- 2-3 coffee meetings worth of value
- Can expense as "software tools"

**Annual Discount (20%):**
- Pro: $468/year ($39/month equivalent) - Save $120
- Improves cash flow
- Reduces churn
- Rewards commitment

**Free → Pro Conversion Triggers:**
- Hit 1,000 request limit (usage-based upgrade)
- Need custom rules (feature-based upgrade)
- Add team members (collaboration-based upgrade)
- Compliance requirements (audit logs, exports)

**Deal Size:**
- Small Enterprise (100-500 employees): $500-1K/month

## 📊 Key Metrics to Track

### Product Metrics
- **Daily/Monthly Active Users (DAU/MAU)**
- **Requests per user** (usage depth)
- **Activation rate** (% who create first firewall)
- **Time to first value** (should be < 5 min)

### Growth Metrics
- **Signups per week/month**
- **Traffic sources** (organic, paid, referral)
- **Conversion rate** (visitor → signup → paid)
- **Viral coefficient** (users referred / user)

### Revenue Metrics
- **MRR/ARR** (Monthly/Annual Recurring Revenue)
- **ARPU** (Average Revenue Per User)
- **CAC** (Customer Acquisition Cost) - target < $100
- **LTV** (Lifetime Value) - target > $500
- **LTV:CAC ratio** - target 3:1 or higher
- **Churn rate** - target < 5%/month

### Unit Economics
```
Pro Customer:
- ARPU: $49/month
- LTV (18 months average): $882
- CAC: $80
- LTV:CAC: 11:1 ✅

Enterprise Customer:
- ARPU: $1,500/month
- LTV (36 months average): $54,000
- CAC: $2,000
- LTV:CAC: 27:1 ✅
```
